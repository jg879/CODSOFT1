AB de Villiers

» Abraham Benjamin de Villiers, commonly known as AB de Villiers, is a retired South African cricketer who is considered one of the greatest batsmen of all time. He was born on February 17, 1984, in Pretoria, South Africa.

» During his cricketing career, AB de Villiers earned a reputation for his unorthodox and innovative batting style. He was equally proficient in all formats of the game and was known for his ability to score at a remarkable pace while maintaining a high level of consistency.

» AB de Villiers represented South Africa in international cricket from 2004 to 2018.Apart from his batting prowess,he was also an exceptional fielder and a part-time wicketkeeper. His athleticism and agility on the field earned him the nickname "Mr. 360".

» In May 2018, AB de Villiers announced his retirement from all forms of international cricket, leaving behind a legacy that will be remembered for generations to come.
